Understanding Alzheimer's Disease

Posted on May 14, 2025

What is Alzheimer's Disease?

Alzheimer's disease is a progressive neurological disorder that causes brain cells to degenerate and die, leading to memory loss and cognitive decline.

Symptoms and Stages

Early symptoms include difficulty remembering recent events or conversations. As the disease progresses, symptoms become more severe.

Current Research

Researchers are working to understand the causes of Alzheimer's and develop treatments to stop or slow its progression.
